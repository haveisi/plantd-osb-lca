# Methodology

## Purpose

This project develops a screening-level comparison between Plantd structural panels and conventional OSB structural panels.

## Functional unit

1 m² of structural panel used for wall or roof sheathing.

## System boundary

Cradle-to-gate screening boundary.

## Data sources

- Plantd structural panel engineering report
- Bio-based construction panel LCA literature
- Natural vs conventional building assembly LCA literature

## Current model status

This is a screening model, not a verified comparative LCA.

## Key limitation

The Plantd report provides structural and code-compliance data but does not provide a full life cycle inventory.

Missing Plantd data include:

- panel mass per m²
- binder/resin type and percentage
- manufacturing electricity
- thermal energy for drying/pressing
- inbound transport distances
- packaging
- end-of-life assumptions

## Current screening assumptions

OSB baseline range:

- Low: 20 kg CO2e/m²
- Mid: 25 kg CO2e/m²
- High: 30 kg CO2e/m²

Plantd screening range:

- Low: 10 kg CO2e/m²
- Mid: 14 kg CO2e/m²
- High: 18 kg CO2e/m²

## Correct interpretation

The current results should be interpreted as an early screening estimate only. They should not be used as verified product claims.
